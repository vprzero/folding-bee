Questions

Good things to know.

Which neighborhoods do you serve?+

We currently serve Towson, Ruxton, and Roland Park, Maryland. Keeping our service area tight is how we stay reliable and give every home the same dedicated Bee. Just outside these neighborhoods? Reach out — we are growing carefully.

Do you wash the laundry?+

Our focus is folding, organizing, and resetting your space. If you'd like washing included in your visit, just mention it when you book and we'll tailor the service to your home.

Do you put everything away?+

Yes — that's the whole point. Everything is folded, sorted, and returned to its place, with your drawers and closets fully reset.

What should I do before you arrive?+

Nothing at all. Clean or unfolded, sorted or complete chaos — we take it from there.

How long does a visit take?+

Most homes take two to four hours depending on volume. After your first visit we'll give you a clear sense of what your home typically needs.

Do you offer ongoing service?+

Yes. Weekly and bi-weekly slots are available, and VIP members lock in a standing time with the same Bee every week.

Is my home and privacy respected?+

Always. We're discreet, careful, fully insured, and we treat your home exactly as we'd treat our own.
